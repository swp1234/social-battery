About the Social Battery Test

How the Real-Time Battery Works

This test presents 12 social scenarios ranging from surprise parties to solo hikes. For each situation, you choose whether it charges or drains your energy. Your on-screen battery reacts in real time, giving you an instant visual of your social energy patterns.

Understanding Social Energy

Social energy varies from person to person. Some people thrive in large groups while others prefer quiet solitude. Neither is better or worse -- understanding your social battery helps you plan your time, set boundaries, and avoid social burnout.
